Return Policy

Biopex is committed to customer satisfaction and aims to handle returns in a fair, transparent, and efficient manner. This Return Policy explains the conditions under which products may be returned, exchanged, or refunded when purchased from Biopex through its website, sales team, or authorized channels. By placing an order with Biopex, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Biopex accepts return requests for most standard laboratory and medical equipment within a specified time frame from the date of delivery, provided that the products are unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging with all accessories, manuals, and documentation intact. The usual return window, any applicable restocking fees, and product categories that qualify or do not qualify for return should be clearly defined by Biopex on its website or order documentation, and customers are encouraged to review those details before making a purchase.

To initiate a return, customers should contact Biopex using the contact information provided on the website or order invoice, supplying order details, product information, and the reason for the return request. Biopex will review the request and provide instructions, including return authorization, packaging guidelines, and the address for returning goods where applicable. Returns sent without prior authorization or with incomplete information may be delayed or refused. Unless otherwise stated, customers are generally responsible for arranging and paying for return shipping, ensuring that products are securely packaged to prevent damage in transit.

Upon receipt of returned products, Biopex will inspect the items to verify their condition and eligibility under this policy. If the return is approved, Biopex may issue a refund, replacement, or credit note, depending on the circumstances and the customer’s preference where options are available. Refunds are usually processed using the original method of payment within a reasonable period after inspection. In some cases, restocking fees, original shipping charges, and associated costs may be deducted from the refund, except where the return is due to an error by Biopex or to defective or damaged goods.

If a product is received damaged, defective, or not as described, customers should notify Biopex promptly within the specified reporting period, providing relevant details and, where possible, photographs or supporting evidence. Biopex may offer repair, replacement, or full refund depending on the assessment and warranty terms. This Return Policy operates alongside any applicable statutory consumer rights and manufacturer warranties and does not limit any rights that cannot be excluded under law.

Biopex reserves the right to update or modify this Return Policy from time to time to reflect changes in its processes, legal requirements, or business practices. The latest version will be made available on the Biopex website, and the policy in effect at the time of purchase will generally apply to that transaction. Customers are encouraged to review this policy periodically and to contact Biopex for clarification if any part of the return process or eligibility criteria is unclear.
